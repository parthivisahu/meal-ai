# Groq AI Models (2026 Edition)

## ✅ Active & Recommended

### 1. **`llama-3.1-8b-instant`** (The Best for Speed)
*   **Best For:** High-volume tasks, simple logic, JSON formatting, price matching.
*   **Verdict:** **Use this for the Price Scraper.**

### 2. **`llama-3.3-70b-versatile`** (The Best for Intelligence)
*   **Best For:** Complex meal planning, reasoning, generating recipes.
*   **Verdict:** **Use this for the Meal Planner.**

### 3. **`deepseek-r1-distill-llama-70b`** (New in 2026 - High Logic)
*   **Best For:** Deep reasoning, math, and complex constraint solving.
*   **Verdict:** Great alternative to Llama 3.3 for the Meal Planner if you need "thinking" capabilities.

### 4. **`qwen-2.5-32b`** (Excellent Balance)
*   **Best For:** Coding, detailed instructions, and structured data.
*   **Verdict:** Good middle-ground between the 8B and 70B models.

### 5. **`gemma2-9b-it`** (Google's Model)
*   **Best For:** Creative writing and friendly conversational tones.
*   **Verdict:** Useful for the "Reasoning" text in your meal plans to make it sound more natural.

### 6. **`llama-3.2-3b-preview`** (Ultra Lightweight)
*   **Best For:** Mobile devices or extremely fast, simple text filtering.
*   **Verdict:** Use if you need even faster responses than the 8B model.
